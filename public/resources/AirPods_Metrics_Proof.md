# Phase 4: Product Metrics & Success Validation

**Project:** AirPods CustomFit Pro (eCornell Product Management)
**Core Competency:** Defining KPIs & North Star Alignment.

## The Metrics Evolution

I applied the **Cornell Tech ctech105** (Understand Common Metrics) framework to move beyond vanity sales data and into sustainable business health.

## Proof of Iteration: From Lagging to Leading

### Iteration 1: The Sales-Centric Approach

* **Initial Metric:** Units Sold.
* **Problem:** High returns on custom products would erase profit margins and destroy brand equity.

### Iteration 2: The "North Star" Realization (Iterated)

* **New Metric:** **"Fit satisfaction Index (FSI)" > 9.0**.
* **Leading Indicator:** "Successful AR Seal Completion" (Users who correctly use the AR app are 95% less likely to return the product).
* **Lagging Indicator:** Return Rate per 100 units.

## Final Success Framework

* **Business Lever:** Reducing "Rework Costs" through software-guided precision.
* **North Star:** "Zero-Return Anatomical Seal" (The moment the customer puts them in and doesn't adjust them for 4 hours).
