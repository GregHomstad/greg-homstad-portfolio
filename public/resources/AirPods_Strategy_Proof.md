# Phase 2: Strategy & User Experience Mapping

**Project:** AirPods CustomFit Pro (eCornell Product Management)
**Core Competency:** Journey Mapping & Behavioral Insights.

## Strategic Goal

To bridge the gap between "purchasing a product" and "trusting a solution" through iterative digital-to-hardware service design.

## Proof of Work: Iterative Journey Transformation

### The "Pain" Baseline

Early mapping showed the primary drop-off point wasn't the price—it was the **anxiety of fit**. Users were hesitant to order custom-molded parts without "trying them on."

### The Strategic Pivot: The "Virtual Fit Check"

I introduced a **Software-Hardware Hybrid strategy** (Module 1: Detailed Template Analysis).

* **The Idea:** Use the iPhone's TrueDepth camera to simulate the "Anatomical Seal" before the order is finalized.
* **Impact:** This moved the product from a "Hardware sales" model to a "Trust-based service" model.

## Journey Evidence

* **User Emotion Iteration:** From "Concerned about loss/comfort" to "Confident in personalized tech."
* **Touchpoint Innovation:** The AR simulation became the "Aha! Moment" in the customer journey.
